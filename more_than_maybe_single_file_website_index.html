More Than Maybe

A heartfelt romance by Gift Aghaulor — love, distance, and the courage to choose.

About the Book

Emma is a guarded artist. Alex is an ambitious professional. A chance meeting changes everything — but when a career-defining job takes Alex abroad, their fragile relationship is tested by distance, doubt, and the pull of ambition. More than Maybe is a story about choosing love despite fear.

What readers love

  • Slow-burn romance with emotional truth
  • Realistic portrayal of distance & career choices
  • Strong, hopeful ending without fairy-tale gloss
